Our Story

Here at Adopt a Unicorn Non-Profit, we believe every family deserves a magical addition to their household. Our mission is to provide loving homes to unicorns in need of a forever family. We have partnered with certified unicorn breeders across the world to bring you the happiest and healthiest unicorns. With every adoption, we donate a portion of the proceeds to various charities dedicated to unicorn conservation and protection.

How to Adopt

Adopting a unicorn has never been easier! Simply follow these steps:

  1. Fill out our adoption application form.
  2. Schedule a virtual meet-and-greet with your unicorn.
  3. Complete the adoption paperwork.
  4. Get ready to welcome your magical new family member home!

About Us

Adopt a Unicorn Non-Profit was founded by a group of unicorn enthusiasts who wanted to make a positive impact on the world of unicorn breeding and conservation. Our team is dedicated to providing a safe and loving environment for all unicorns, and to educating the public on the unique needs and challenges of unicorn ownership. We believe every family deserves a chance to experience the magic of unicorn ownership, and we are committed to making that dream a reality for as many people as possible.
